Project discription : This project is a web based shopping system for an existing shop. Online shopping is a form of electronic commerce which allows consumers to directly buy goods or services from a seller over the Internet using a the web-based application. Consumers find a product of interest by visiting the webiste of the retailer directly or by searching among alternative vendors using a shopping search engine, which displays the same product's availability and pricing at different e-retailers. As of 2020, customers can shop online using a range of different computers and devices, including desktop computers, laptops, tablet computers and smartphones.
